Powering the Industrial AI Revolution

We build the brain for the world's machines. Noval AI connects state-of-the-art generative models to the physical reality of manufacturing.

Our Mission

Manufacturing generates more data than any other industry, yet 90% of it goes unused. We are changing that.

By fine-tuning Large Multimodal Models (LMMs) on proprietary industrial data, we enable factories to automate complex visual tasks, predict failures before they happen, and optimize production lines autonomously.

We believe the future of making things is intelligent, autonomous, and self-correcting.
